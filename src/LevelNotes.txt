Level Notes

0 - ground level
	grass is most common
	some rock can be expected
	some dirt patches can be expected
	there is a x% chance that grass will extend up to form a hill
	there is a x% chance that rock will continue if next to another rock
	there is a x% chance that rock will descend down into rock deposits
	
-1 to -3 below ground level
	the chances of dirt versus rock become more even
	small chance of coal
	smaller chance of iron
	
-4 to -7 below below ground
	the chances of rock outweigh dirt
	there is a chance of openings in the rock (caves)
